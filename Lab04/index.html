Coco Palm Dhuni Kolhu - Maldives - overview introduction

Property Overview

Coco Palm Dhuni Kolhu occupies a private island in the Baa Atoll of the Maldives . Nestled on the Equator, this unique island nation enjoys warm tropical weather throughout the year. On Dhuni Kolhu Island the ivory white beaches encircle a lush tropical paradise overlooking a breathtaking lagoon of unparalleled beauty. This gem of an island is widely acclaimed as one of the most beautiful islands in the region. Your 30 minute seaplane transfer to Coco Palm Dhuni Kolhu is an experience in itself as after takeoff from Male you will be regaled with a breathtaking view of the Maldives from the air, where its true colors and beauty are revealed.

Location

Coco Palm Dhuni Kolhu is located in Baa Atoll in the Maldives. Lying over the Equator. Transfer to the island is 30 minutes by sea plane from Male’ International Airport, which offers breathtaking aerial views of coral islands.

Airport Transfer

Transfer time between the airport and the resort is about 35 minutes by seaplane. Night transfer is not provided. Airport transfer modes available: Sea plane transfer.

Coco Palm Dhuni Kolhu - Maldives - Accommodation Room Villa Suites

Accommodation Overview

On Coco Palm Dhuni Kolhu, we understand that during your time with us, you need to feel at home. The detailing on our villas ensures superior comfort and heightened intimacy. Blending western comforts with local culture our open air garden bathrooms promise total privacy. Our accommodation offers a wide choice of features depending on your preference. These include splash pools with private terraces, sunken or spa baths either in the garden or over the lagoon and king size or tropical style four poster beds. The finely crafted wood and rattan furnishings in our villas with their pure cotton accessories have been designed to enhance nature's beauty and conjure up a truly magical feel. All beach and deluxe villas have beach frontage and the lagoon villas are an alternative experience elevated above the beautiful azure waters of the lagoon.

Beach Villa - (58 rooms / units)

Set slightly back from the beach for privacy, the Beach Villa is a spacious 87sqm offering discerning comfort and appeal with their white walls and warm wood tones throughout. The entrance has a terrace containing comfortable wooden reclining chairs. Enjoy your stay in air conditioned comfort or savor the natural cooling effect of the tropical breeze in the Maldivian tradition
